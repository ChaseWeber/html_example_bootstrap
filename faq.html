Frequently Asked Questions:

  • What types of solar systems are available?

    • There are generally three types of solar panels:

    1. Centralized Solar Generator Array

      2. This system uses multiple small panels to shine light to a central tower which heats up fluid to run a generator. This kind is typically only used for power plants or large energy generating structures.

    2. Modular Solar Generator Array

      3. This system has multple small panels that each have an individual piping connection to locally heat fluid for a generator. This is more practical for smaller energy generation but still not very practical for office or home installation.

    3. Solar Capture Panels

      4. This style is more common as it is easily integrated into existing power systems and homes. These panels directly convert solar energy to electricity at each panel. This system is much more modular and plug-and-play friendly. The only drawback are the inherent low efficiency ratings of solar-electrical conversion.
